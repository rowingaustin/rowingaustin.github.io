EVENT REGISTRATION

Join us for our annual event ride "Cycle the Continental Divide" which benefits our local children's hospital. Cycling teams and families of all skill levels are welcome to join. This annual event is a favorite for our community and is supported by numerous volunteers. Whether you ride a bike or not, we welcome your support. All proceeds benefit Children's Hospital, Bozeman.

Event Informaton

This cycling journey takes place July 10-13, 2018 with routes starting ending in Bozeman. Mapped trails include a simplie six-mile ride from the city center to Sourdough Canyon Road, a former logging route with views of Saddle Peak. Other more difficult routes are available for our experienced riders. Initial registration costs are $100 but we encourage all riders to raise more funds to donate to the cause.
